Planet Express

Developed a Wordmark and Brandmark for a Commercial spaceport



For this project, I was assigned to take a fictional company and develop both a wordmark and brandmark for them. I chose Futurama’s Planet Express and reimagined it as a commercial spaceline. My focus was to create a modern yet thematic design, similar to the branding seen in today’s airline logos. It was important to maintain a contemporary look while still capturing the essence of the original concept, ensuring that the brand identity felt relevant and cohesive.
