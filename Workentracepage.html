Harbrew

I designed a wordmark and brandmark for a fantasy brand that uses alchemy to blend the harmony of beer and music. The logo needed to be versatile across various media, with both the wordmark and brandmark able to stand alone. For the brandmark, I combined a vinyl record and a potion bottle to create a logo that reflects the original concept.
