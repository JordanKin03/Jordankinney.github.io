The Vapor Collective

Designed a mock logo for a non-profit clothing brand.



The Vapor Collective is a non-profit organization I made to support the fight against air pollution. Like many organizations, it requires funding, and its approach is through a clothing brand. The logo draws inspiration from companies like Nike and Adidas, featuring a simple vector design that’s easily adaptable to various colors. The logo’s design incorporates a sun and cloud, nodding to the theme of air pollution.
